Vance drew inspiration from a variety of sources, including fashion magazines, classic films, and even Roberts' own personal style. The result was a wardrobe that is both iconic and timeless.

Some of the most memorable outfits from the film include the white dress that Vivian wears to the opera, the red dress that she wears to the polo match, and the black dress that she wears to the final scene.
